System Roles

Roles contain a set of permissions that grant access to specific system resources.

  • Administrator

    Full system access with all permissions

  • Doctor

    Medical staff with patient management and clinical access

  • Nurse

    Nursing staff with patient care and medication access

  • Receptionist

    Front desk staff with appointment and registration access

  • Pharmacist

    Pharmacy staff with medication and prescription access

  • Lab Technician

    Laboratory staff with test and results access
